# Energy Community Simulation and Optimization Framework
A Python-based simulation framework for modelling and optimizing residential energy communities.
Key Features:
Demand Simulation: Hourly heating, cooling, and baseload electricity demand modelling
Heat Pump Modeling: Simulation of air-source heat pumps electricity demand.
PV Simulation: Hourly PV power generation simulation.
Energy Optimization: optimal scheduling based on MILP.
Equity & Fairness Analysis: Evaluate household-level energy burden.
